Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Introduction

Lume ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using Lume, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Authentication data (Sign in with Apple)
  • Subscription and payment information (processed by Apple)

Usage Data

  • Journal entries and reflections (encrypted and stored securely)
  • Dopamine mapping data and activities
  • Habit tracking information
  • App usage patterns and preferences

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and crash logs
  • Anonymous analytics data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Lume app
  • Personalize your experience with relevant prompts and insights
  • Process your subscription and transactions
  • Send you notifications and reminders (with your permission)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
  • Journal entries are stored with AES-256 encryption
  • We use secure authentication methods including Sign in with Apple
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access to personal data is strictly limited to authorized personnel

Your journal entries and personal reflections are private. We do not read, sell, or share your personal content with third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can:

  • Export your data at any time from the app settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request data deletion by contacting support

Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

Lume may use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure data storage and authentication
  • Apple App Store: For subscription processing
  • Apple Analytics: For anonymous app performance data

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@lumeapp.com

Children's Privacy

Lume is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification for significant changes

Your continued use of Lume after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
